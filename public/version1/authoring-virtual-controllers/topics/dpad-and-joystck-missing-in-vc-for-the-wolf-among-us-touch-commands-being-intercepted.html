> -Buttons seem to show up on screen, but a d-pad or joystick does not, no matter where I place it. Opacity is the same as the buttons. I'm just trying to bind something to WASD.

Would you please submit a bug report and be sure to include your debug logs. You can find the bug form here: https://gameplay.gestureworks.com/bug_reports/new/

> -Should touchscreen taps still be processed by the game? It seems like the gameplay overlay is consuming all of the events, and nothing is making it to the game.

Yes Gameplay is consuming all of the events. If you would like a specific behavior from your touch events, you can create your very own Global Gesture to do this for you.

> -Is there documentation for the settings? I'm wondering whether "use mouse queue" or "enable 2 in 1 mode" would do anything here.

With respect to mouse queue and keyboard queue, instead of being fired as soon as they are received, events are put into a queue and processed at a regular interval. These settings are enabled by default, as they offer the widest compatibility with applications. Enable 2-in-1 is an experimental feature that is geared towards 2 in 1 devices. It automatically hides a virtual controller when in laptop mode and appears when in tablet mode.
