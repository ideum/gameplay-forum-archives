Ohh, yes, you have to do all the mapping of buttons while you're in the Gameplay application, not while you're in game. The only editing you can do to your Virtual Controller in game is to resize the buttons or move them around. What we've sometimes done is look up the in-game controls online. For instance, you can Google "The Darkness 2 PC controls," and you may have some luck finding the keyboard keys for each action to help you out. Otherwise, if you wanted to keep your game open to look at the controls, you could also Alt+Tab between applications.

We are definitely planning on having some video tutorials in the near future! :) Thanks so much for pointing this out and for your question!
