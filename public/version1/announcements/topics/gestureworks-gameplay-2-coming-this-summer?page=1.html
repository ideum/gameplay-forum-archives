Hey guys, thanks for the responses and questions!

As outlined in the blog post regarding Gameplay 2, beta will start in May (sorry, not in time for your flight!). Anyone who has submitted a Virtual Controller for a game in Gameplay 1 and has gotten their controller approved will receive access to beta, along with a free upgrade to Gameplay 2! Therefore, if you would like to receive this software for free once it is released, all you need to do is make a controller and upload it and have the controller approved. Another option too to receive a free upgrade is to simply just be active and helpful in the community on these forums. :)

As with most types of software, in order to upgrade to the newest version, you must purchase it (usually at a discount if you owned the previous version). Instead of this discount though, we are allowing anyone with just a bit of initiative to upgrade for completely free and to also even be able to participate in beta just by having submitted a Virtual Controller that gets approved (or just being active on the forums). Regardless of when you made a controller, whether it was awhile back ago or more recently, as long as it's been approved, you will receive a copy of Gameplay 2. :)
