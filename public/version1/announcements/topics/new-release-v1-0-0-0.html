marsh

That's right everyone, we've gone gold! The 1.0 release of GestureWorks Gameplay is now available for download (and purchase!) on the Gameplay site.

Everyone on the GestureWorks team would like to extend their thanks to all of you who posted in the forums, filed bug reports, and worked with us to make GestureWorks Gameplay the solid product that it is. We have the utmost appreciation for your efforts, and while we were of course not able to implement every one of your suggestions in the time allotted, we are proud to have come so far in such a short development cycle.

Rest assured that we've recorded all of your feedback thus far, and we've got quite a list of feature requests and improvements already on the list for possible inclusion in future releases. Also, keep those suggestions and forum postings coming - those shouldn't end just because the Beta has.

Lastly, please be sure to spread the word about GestureWorks Gameplay - the bigger the success of the product, the more improvements we can make.

Thanks again, everyone!

--Marsh

ps. We're still working out a few kinks on the new public site and appreciate your patience while they're ironed out. This includes adding a link to the bug form back in since we still want to hear from you on those - stay tuned!
