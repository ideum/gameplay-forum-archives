  - In reply to Existentialism
 

Hi, Existentialism. We are discussing a feature of Gestureworks Gameplay that allows user the option to "Autostart Game Clients". Be default, GWG will open all the game clients you own on start-up (whether you already have those clients set to open on start-up automatically or not). For people who use their computers for things besides gaming, this could get annoying after awhile, which is why we added the feature.

Having Autostart unchecked, however, could be problematic with certain games that need Steam to open by Gameplay on start-up (such as Terraria and Assassins Creed). To fix this, all you'd have to do is check "Auto Start-up" again, sign out, and sign back in.

Some games need to be launched with Steam, some don't. Most just launch directly through the Gameplay tool. For a full list of launch instructions see this board: https://gameplay.gestureworks.com/gameplay_forums/forums/game-specific-issues/topics/how-to-launch-instructions
