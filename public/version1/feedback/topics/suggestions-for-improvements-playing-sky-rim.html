So I'm travelling on the road today so I thought I'd fire up and play sky rim for a couple hours. After an hour or so I've come up with a couple of suggestions that may improve the experience for sky rim at least, among other games as we'll.

First a double tap command for the d-pad that would enable the sprint command (alt modifier). I found trying to sprint and navigate the movement and was almost impossible as I felt I needed a third hand.

Second, a tap command on the joystick mapped to the left mouse click. Fighting off foes was difficult due to moving from the joystick to the nominated button mapped to the left mouse click. I tried setting a gesture for tapping on the screen and mapped it to the left mouse button but I got very strange results ( and in fact the gesture would not work if I tapped over the joystick)

If I think of more I will continue to post here. Keep up the great work guys, the world has no idea just how powerful your product already is, and just how much more powerful it will become.

Hey there, srbaude! Thanks so much for testing out Gameplay for us while you're on the road and also for the suggestions! Much appreciated. :)

It is quite possible to map a double tap Global Gesture to any action in game, such as sprint or attack. However, with games like Skyrim, where the mouse is used as the camera/you move your mouse around to move the camera, any tapping on the screen is automatically recognized as moving the camera in that direction, so that's why you're getting some strange results from that. We found, because of the game, it doesn't work well to have Global Gestures other than a Drag mapped to Mouse Look (in order to drag your finger anywhere on screen to move the camera around).

Thank you again for your feedback, and I look forward to hearing more from you on your impressions of Gameplay!
