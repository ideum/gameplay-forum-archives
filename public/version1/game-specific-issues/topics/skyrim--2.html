Hi ReapersMinion,

We've found that sometimes when launching Skyrim, the game itself chooses a resolution for you if you don't select your own preferred resolution in the launcher window. Next time you try playing the game, double check what resolution setting you're on from the launcher. Let us know if changing the resolution here in game still does not help your situation.

As for the software not updating, it would have been really helpful if you could have submitted a bug report and attached your log files (found through a link from the "i" icon in the top right-hand corner of Gameplay) so that we could have taken a look at just what exactly was causing that issue. I apologize that happened to you though, and we'll try reproducing that here to see if we can fix that from happening to anyone else.
