acousticguru

  - In reply to pauliusval99
 

Remember that any desired touch behavior has to be explicitly designed via the tool. If you want the ability to use a single finger to tap on menu items, you will need to create a Global Gesture. Assign the output of a 1 finger Tap to the left mouse button and wherever you tap, a left mouse click will take place at the location you tap. I confirmed that this works for navigating menus in Black Mesa. When you are done creating controls for these awesome games be sure to upload them to the community. Looking forward to seeing your Virtual Controllers!
