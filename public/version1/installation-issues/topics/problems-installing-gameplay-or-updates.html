Hey there, ratteh!

It sounds like you may be experiencing some known issues that some people may be experiencing. The good news is that it is easily fixed! Simply do a complete clean uninstall:

  1. First, make sure any game clients you may have (such as Steam or Origin) are closed. Press the Windows button + R on your keyboard to bring up the Run command. Type in %appdata% and delete the Ideum folder entirely. Press Windows + R again and this time type in %programdata% and delete the Ideum folder in there.

  2. Press Windows + R and type: regedit Hit Yes to the pop-up box. HOWEVER! Please be extremely careful not to delete anything else other than what follows, otherwise your computer may be seriously damanged. First, expand/double click on HKEY_CURRENT_USER -> Software -> Ideum -> GestureWorks -> Gameplay. Then delete your LicenseKey from that list. (Make sure you have it written down or saved somewhere. You should have received an e-mail with your license key/verification code in it for your records.)

  3. Open the Charms Bar to go to your Settings and Control Panel. Under Programs and Features, Uninstall GestureWorks Gameplay. - See more at: https://gameplay.gestureworks.com/gameplay_forums/forums/installation-issues/topics/how-to-clean-install-of-gwg#sthash.e1yd064x.dpuf

Then, to reinstall the software, you'll need to download it directly off our website, which can be found here via the Download Beta image with the green "Get It Now" button: https://gameplay.gestureworks.com/virtual_controllers

Just install that, restart your computer after the updates finish installing, and you should be good to go! Let us know if the error messages persist.

As for your problem with the trial version still appearing - did you restart your computer at all? After entering in your license key, the only way to update Gameplay to the actual version and not the trial version is to do a restart. Hopefully the clean install will fix the rendering issue, allowing you to see all of the buttons. If not though, that's a bug that needs to be reported in our bug form here: https://gameplay.gestureworks.com/bug_reports/new/

Thanks so much for your detailed response! We greatly appreciate it and hope this solves your issues!
