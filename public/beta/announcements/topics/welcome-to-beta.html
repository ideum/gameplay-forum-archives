Here are some quick tips on getting started with Gameplay 2 Beta:

  1. Backup your Virtual Controllers before installing V2. Do this first since opening V2 with a VC.XML from V1 in your %appdata% folder will upgrade them to V2 specs, and this is a one-way upgrade. You may want to preserve your VCs just in case you decide to roll back to V1, and if you do you'll need this backup.

  2. Read the README.rtf! Then read the README_REMOTE.TXT in the installation directory if you wish to use the Android Remote feature. This is a big feature that includes a few steps needed to get going so you'll want to familiarize yourself with the how-to of it all.

  3. If you submit a bug, submit all of your logs as well as your virtual_controllers.xml along with it. This really, really helps us determine the cause of and create fixes for your bugs.

  4. Be patient. :) This is a beta product and while we have tried to ensure all major features are functional, you may (and likely will) find things that don't work quite right. That's what this is for - finding and fixing the broken stuff.

Thanks in advance for your help, everyone! We're looking forward to working with all of you to make this release a solid one.

--Marsh
