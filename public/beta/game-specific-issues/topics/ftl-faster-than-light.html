A bit of a weird one this....

My Test Device: Asus T100 32GB

I spotted & tried the existing profile uploaded by someone else (sorry, forget who). It has lots of buttons for various things, but it doesn't have any global gesture mappings to interact with the menus/game/etc. So, I added a simple 'Tap' gesture mapped to left-click.

Unfortunately, the mouse control is unusable with this title. Having said that, I think when I tried simply touching the screen to control the FTL menus without GWG running, it behaved in a similar way (or at least, it wasn't clicking where I was touching!)

What seems to be happening is that when you tap your finger near what I'll call the 'hotspot' (maybe slightly left of centre on the screen), there's a very sensitive zone where each tap slightly moves the mouse cursor on the screen in the same direction you tap away from that 'hotspot'. As well as moving the cursor, it also performs the left-click - but obviously in an unpredictable position on the screen!

So you can kind of shift the cursor clumsily in the direction you want, but it's not much use to control the game. I can't even manage to successfully navigate the menus!

FWIW the buttons in the virtual controller seem to function, although I'm not sure what most of them are for... not played much of FTL yet!

Hopefully someone has had more success with this game since there's a virtual controller that's been uploaded. Any clues?

Thanks!

Quick question, if you have it on border less full screen are you still getting the weird mouse movement?

With that octodad controller scheme I found the game to work properly however one thing that I found difficult was the mouse cursor position. It is located under your finger which makes it hard to see what your clicking on.

If I use tap plus drag I get the same double click problem you are having. A solution to this I found was to put the tap input to left mouse click, then put a modifier on the same tap gesture for another left mouse click. I did not play very far into the game to see if any issues arise from this, but in all the in-game menus (jump/ship/etc) I am not running into the double tap thing anymore. Hope that helps!
